Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a new game?

Simply launch the app and tap on "Start Game" from the main menu. You can choose your preferred game settings before beginning.

Can I play offline?

Yes! Kek Huay is designed to work completely offline. No internet connection is required to enjoy the game.

How do I change the language?

You can change the language by tapping the language button in the main menu and selecting your preferred language from the list.

Is my game progress saved?

Yes, your game progress is automatically saved on your device. You can continue your game anytime by selecting "Continue" from the main menu.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please email us at waramitystudio@gmail.com with a description of the bug and any relevant details about when it occurred. Screenshots are helpful if possible.
